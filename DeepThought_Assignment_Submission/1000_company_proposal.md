# DeepThought Assignment – Part B

## Proposal for Building a Database of 1000 ICP-Qualified Companies

### Objective

The goal is to build a verified database of 1000 Indian companies that match DeepThought's Ideal Customer Profile (Federer companies) within one month. The focus will be on quality rather than quantity, ensuring every shortlisted company is supported by evidence.

---

## Phase 1 – Build the Initial Company Universe

I would first create a pool of around 3500–4000 companies because not every company will qualify after verification.

The initial database would be collected from multiple reliable sources:

* DSIR Recognized R&D Companies
* MCA Company Database
* GST Portal
* BSE SME & NSE Emerge
* Industry Associations (CHEMEXCIL, CII, FICCI, GCCI)
* TradeIndia
* IndiaMART
* LinkedIn Sales Navigator
* Export databases such as Volza and Zauba
* Industry exhibitions like ChemExpo India, CPHI India, BioAsia, PlastIndia and India Chem.

Using multiple sources reduces the chances of missing high-quality companies.

---

## Phase 2 – Automated Qualification

Once the master list is prepared, I would automate the first screening process.

For every company I would collect:

* Website
* Product portfolio
* Manufacturing location
* Leadership information
* Certifications
* News
* Hiring activity
* Revenue indicators

AI tools like Claude and Gemini would summarize the information while Python scripts would organize the collected data.

Companies would automatically be rejected if they are:

* Traders or distributors
* Pure service providers
* Revenue above ₹500 Cr
* PE-controlled or subsidiaries
* No active website
* No visible activity in recent years

---

## Phase 3 – Human Verification

Automation cannot completely replace judgment.

Therefore every shortlisted company would be manually verified using:

* Company Website
* LinkedIn
* MCA filings
* GST Portal
* Annual Reports
* Industry News
* Google Search

Borderline companies would receive additional review before inclusion.

---

## Phase 4 – Quality Control

To maintain consistency, every company would be checked against the six Federer criteria:

* Differentiated Products
* Decision Maker Quality
* Growing Sector
* Growth Signals
* Systems Maturity
* Leadership Succession

Each company would receive evidence-backed scores before being accepted.

---

## Weekly Timeline

### Week 1

Build a universe of approximately 4000 companies using government databases, industry directories and trade associations.

### Week 2

Collect company information through websites, AI-assisted research and public databases.

### Week 3

Verify shortlisted companies manually and remove false positives.

### Week 4

Prepare the final verified database of approximately 1000 companies, organize personalization notes and perform quality checks.

---

## Expected Funnel

* Initial Companies: 4000
* After Basic Filters: 2500
* After AI Qualification: 1500
* After Manual Verification: 1000

---

## Conclusion

My approach combines automation with human verification. AI significantly reduces research time while manual validation ensures the final database is accurate, evidence-based and useful for DeepThought's sales team. This balance allows quality to remain high even while scaling research across thousands of companies.
